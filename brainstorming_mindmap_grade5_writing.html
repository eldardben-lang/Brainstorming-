What is brainstorming?A creative thinking technique where you quickly write down ALL your ideas without judging them — the more ideas, the better! No wrong answers.
✍️
Why writers brainstorm!Even famous authors brainstorm before writing. It unlocks imagination, helps you find the BEST idea, and saves you from staring at a blank page!
Brainstorming
What Is Brainstorming? 💡
The art of letting your imagination run wild — no limits, no wrong answers!
The rules of brainstorming
✅
Write everything down — even silly ideas!
✅
No judging — every idea is valid at this stage
✅
More is better — aim for quantity first
✅
Build on ideas — one idea leads to another
❌
Don't stop to edit — just keep flowing!
What makes a GREAT story idea?
👤
Interesting character — someone readers care about
⚡
A problem or conflict — something to solve
🌍
A vivid setting — somewhere exciting or unusual
❤️
Emotion — something the reader can FEEL
🔄
Change — the character learns or grows
The writing process — brainstorming comes FIRST!
1. Brainstorm 💡 → 2. Plan 🗺️ → 3. Draft ✍️ → 4. Revise 🔄 → 5. Publish 📖
✨
Famous authors brainstorm too!J.K. Rowling sketched Harry Potter's world on napkins. Roald Dahl kept notebooks full of random ideas. Brainstorming is where ALL great stories begin!
Mind Maps
What Is a Mind Map? 🗺️
A visual thinking tool — your ideas branch out like a tree from one central topic!
How to build a mind map
1
Write your main topic in the centre (e.g. "My Story")
2
Draw main branches for each big idea (character, setting, etc.)
3
Add sub-branches with details and ideas for each branch
4
Use colours and pictures to make it visual and memorable
The 5 branches for a story mind map
👤 Character 🌍 Setting ⚡ Problem ❤️ Theme 🏁 Ending
Each branch represents a key story element. Fill in details on the sub-branches to build your complete story idea!
💡
Key idea!A mind map lets your brain think non-linearly — making unexpected connections between ideas. That's where the most original, creative story ideas come from!
